POTA (Parks on the Air) Map by EA7KLK

POTA Map by Volker Kerkhoff, EA7KLK, Montequinto, Spain. All data provided by Openstreetmap and its community. The accuracy and data of this map depends on volunteers maintaining the data. If you are a regular participant in the POTA program, consider contributing. Instructions on how to do that in OpenStreetMap will follow soon.
